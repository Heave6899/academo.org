Spectrogram

A project between Edward Ball at Academo (UK) and Prof. Michael J. Ruiz at UNC Asheville (USA), based on work by Boris Smus, Google (USA).

We have developed an online spectrogram program with a bank of over 30 audio clips to visualize a variety of sounds. Our audio library includes everyday sounds such as speech, singing, musical instruments, birds, a baby, cat, dog, sirens, a jet, thunder, and screaming. We provide a link to a video of the sound sources superimposed with their respective spectrograms in real time. Readers can use our spectrogram program to view our library, open their own desktop audio files, and use the program in real time with a computer microphone.
